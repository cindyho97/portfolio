Cindy Ho

About Me

My name is Cindy Ho and I am a 25-year-old technology enthusiast. I recently finished my bachelor's degree in Multimedia Technology - Virtual Production at Karel de Grote University College in Belgium.
I truly believe that Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality can help people in many ways.
What I'm most excited about are the endless possibilities these apps can bring
in the health and education sector.
Helping people learn and improve themselves through technology is something I am very passionate about.
I would love to be able to contribute to positive social change worldwide.

What I Do
Unity Development

I have made several projects in Unity, both in 2D and 3D. There's still so much potential in 3D web experiences so I'd love to specialize in WebGL/WebGPU.

3D Modeling

For 3D models, I mainly use Blender but I have used Cinema4D before. To make sure that the 3D models are real-size, I use the MeasureIt Blender add-on.

UX/UI Design

I have used the Figma and Miro design tools for wireframing and prototyping. I also have experience with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and After Effects for editing and animating graphics.

Web Development

Building websites has always been another big interest of mine. I have some experience in Wordpress but would love to experiment with front-end frameworks like React JS to combine this with 3D apps.



Video Editing

I started editing videos as a hobby but noticed that this skill can be useful for so many projects. The software that I use for editing is Adobe Premiere Pro and for screen recordings OBS.

Photography

Taking pictures is something I really enjoy in my spare time. I am an amateur photographer, but I'll take every chance to capture beautiful sceneries around the world and special moments with my friends and family.
