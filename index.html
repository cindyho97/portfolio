Cindy Ho

About Me

My name is Cindy Ho and I am a 20 year old technology enthusiast. I am currently studying Multimedia Technology - Virtual Production at Karel de Grote University College in Belgium.
I truly believe that Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality can help people in many ways.
What I'm most excited about is the endless possibilities these apps can bring in the education sector.
Making learning materials fun and immersive, will drastically change the way of learning new things.
I would love to be able to contribute to positive social change worldwide.

What I Do
Unity Developer

I have made several small projects in Unity both in 2D and 3D. I would like to specialize in making Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality apps because I believe that these apps will have a big influence in almost every sector in the future.

3D Artist

3D is used in a lot of games and apps to make it more realistic and immersive for the user. This is why it is important to be able to create or at least have some knowledge about 3D models. The 3D softwares that I use are Cinema 4D and Blender.

Digital Marketing

I am currently enrolled in a program that is created by Google and a Belgian digital marketing company. The students will learn about digital marketing and apply this knowledge to help a NGO. This makes the program very hands-on and therefore I will be able to put it into practice in the next half year.

UI/UX Design

The user has to to have an enjoyable experience while using your application. For this reason I have some experience with UI/UX design in websites and apps. Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator are the softwares that I use.

Photography

Taking pictures is something I really enjoy in my spare time. I am still an amateur photographer, but I'll take every chance to capture beautiful sceneries around the world and special moments with my friends and family.

Piano

During my free time, I also like to play the piano. Even though it can be really frustrating at times, it will always pay off when I keep praciticing and don't give up.
