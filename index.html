About Me

Hi! My name is Cindy Ho and I am a 25-year-old technology enthusiast.

to explore new technologies and learn new skills.

What I Do
Unity Development

I have made several projects in Unity, both in 2D and 3D. My focus is primarily on designing the apps, creating 3D models and implementing UI with programming (C#).

3D Modeling

For 3D models, I mainly use Blender but have used Cinema4D before. To make sure that the 3D models are real-size, I use the MeasureIt Blender add-on.

UX/UI Design

I have used the Figma and Miro design tools for wireframing and prototyping. I also have experience with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator for editing graphics.

Web Development

Building websites has always been another big interest of mine. I have some experience with Wordpress (Elementor & Oxygen Builder). I'm also familiar with HTML, CSS, Javascript and Bootstrap.



Video Editing

I started editing videos as a hobby but noticed that this skill can be useful for so many projects. The software that I use for editing is Adobe Premiere Pro and for screen recordings OBS.

Photography

Taking pictures is something I really enjoy in my spare time. I am an amateur photographer, but I'll take every chance to capture beautiful sceneries around the world and special moments with my friends and family.
